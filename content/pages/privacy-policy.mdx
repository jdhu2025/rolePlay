## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Keepsay** collects, uses, and protects information when you use our AI character chat and fictional roleplay service.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:

- **Account information**: name, email address, login provider, profile details, and account settings.
- **User content**: prompts, messages, custom character details, memory summaries, uploaded images, generated character images, voice requests, and feedback.
- **Usage information**: feature usage, credits, purchases, subscription status, device and browser information, logs, and approximate location from IP address.
- **Payment information**: purchase records, plan, product, amount, currency, payment provider IDs, invoice links, and subscription status. Card details are processed by our payment provider and are not stored by Keepsay.
- **Support information**: messages you send to support and related metadata.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide and improve AI character chat, memory, voice, and image features.
- Authenticate users and secure accounts.
- Process credits, payments, subscriptions, invoices, and cancellations.
- Screen prompts and enforce our Terms and Acceptable Use Policy.
- Prevent abuse, fraud, security incidents, and policy violations.
- Provide customer support.
- Analyze service performance and reliability.

## AI and Moderation

User prompts and related context may be sent to AI providers to generate responses, voice, or images. Prompts for image and video-related generation may also be sent to moderation providers, including Creem, before generation. If a prompt is blocked or moderation is unavailable, the generation request may be denied.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate Keepsay, including payment processors, AI model providers, hosting, storage, email, analytics, moderation, and customer support tools. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect users, Keepsay, or others.

## Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, and resolve disputes. You may request account or data deletion by contacting support.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No internet service is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Choices

You may update account information in settings, cancel subscriptions from billing settings, or contact support to request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data where applicable.

## Children

Keepsay is not intended for children or minors. Users must be at least 18 years old or the age of majority in their jurisdiction.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page.
