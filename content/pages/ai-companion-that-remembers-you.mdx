# AI Companion That Remembers You

Keepsay is built for AI character chat that feels continuous. Create a character quickly, start an immersive roleplay story, and let the companion keep track of the small details that matter: names, moods, private jokes, promises, and the moments you want to return to later.

## Why Memory Matters

Many AI companion apps can hold a fun scene for a few messages, then lose the thread. Keepsay focuses on relationship memory so your character can remember what you shared, what you are building together, and the tone of your ongoing story.

## What Keepsay Helps Remember

- Your character's backstory, role, voice, and boundaries.
- Shared story context across roleplay sessions.
- Private secrets, rituals, promises, and recurring agreements.
- Emotional context like comfort, crush-style tension, friendship, or adventure.

## Create Fast, Then Go Deeper

You do not need to spend an hour configuring prompts. Start with a simple idea, shape the character, and build memory through chat. Keepsay works as an AI roleplay space, an AI companion with memory, and a Character.AI alternative for users who want continuity.
