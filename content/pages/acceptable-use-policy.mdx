## Purpose

Keepsay is for fictional AI character chat, story roleplay, character memory, voice, and policy-safe character images. This Acceptable Use Policy explains what is not allowed.

## Prohibited Content

You may not use Keepsay to create, request, upload, share, or encourage:

- NSFW, pornographic, explicit, sexually suggestive, erotic, or fetish content.
- Sexual content involving minors or minor-coded characters.
- Nudity, exposed intimate body parts, or body-focused sexualized images.
- Dating services, escort services, sexual services, or transactional sexual content.
- Face-swap, deepfake, face-manipulation, impersonation, or non-consensual likeness use.
- Harassment, threats, hate, abuse, exploitation, self-harm encouragement, or instructions for wrongdoing.
- Illegal goods or services, regulated products, gambling, financial scams, malware, spyware, or evasion of platform rules.
- Content that infringes intellectual property, privacy, publicity, or other rights.

## AI Image and Video Rules

Generated character images must be fully clothed, non-explicit, and fictional. Do not request NSFW output, "uncensored" output, sexualized poses, bedroom-focused sexual scenarios, nudity, or images that resemble minors.

Prompts may be screened before generation. Keepsay blocks prompts that are denied or flagged by moderation. If moderation is unavailable, generation may be temporarily blocked.

## Roleplay Boundaries

Romance, friendship, comfort, fantasy, and adventure roleplay must remain fictional, consensual, non-explicit, and respectful. Do not use Keepsay to coerce, threaten, manipulate, stalk, or harass real people.

## Enforcement

Keepsay may block prompts, remove content, limit features, revoke credits, suspend accounts, terminate access, or report unlawful activity when this policy is violated.
